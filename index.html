About The Camp

Six days that will change your life

AIC Jericho Youth Ministry invites all youth to an unforgettable summer camp experience in the beautiful coastal city of Mombasa. From powerful Bible discussions to team challenges and beach adventures — this camp is designed to strengthen your faith, build lasting friendships, and create memories you'll carry for life.

Hosted at Mombasa Baptist High School, the camp runs from August 16th to 21st, 2026. All are welcome — come as you are, leave transformed.

Camp Programme

Six days, packed with purpose

Day 1 — Aug 16
Arrival & Welcome
Check in, settle in, orientation, opening worship & prayer, evening bonfire.
Day 2 — Aug 17
Team Building Day
Morning devotionals, team challenges, trust exercises, afternoon games tournament.
Day 3 — Aug 18
Ocean & Beach Day
Swimming, beach games, sunset worship on the shore.
Day 4 — Aug 19
Bible Deep Dive
Intensive Bible discussions, breakout groups, testimonies, ministry time.
Day 5 — Aug 20
Nature Trail Adventure
Guided nature trail, reflection time, evening talent showcase.
Day 6 — Aug 21
Closing Celebration
Final worship service, commissioning prayer, certificate ceremony, farewell.
